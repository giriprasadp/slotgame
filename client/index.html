INFO & RULES
HOW TO PLAY

Huff and Puff is a 3×5 cascading morph slot with 10 fixed paylines. Match 3 or more identical symbols on a payline from left to right to win. Winning symbols are removed and new ones cascade down — re-triggering wins and advancing the multiplier ladder.

Use BET +/− to set your stake, then press SPIN. Use AUTO to run multiple spins unattended. Enable TURBO for faster animations.

SYMBOLS & PAYTABLE

High-pay symbols — Jackpot Pearl · Free Spin · Bonus · Sea Fish

Low-pay symbols — Fish · Starfish · Plant · A · K · Q · J · 10

Wild (W01): Substitutes for all pay symbols. Own pays: 15× / 40× / 150×

Bursting Wild (W02): Wild that expands 1–4 adjacent cells into W01 after evaluating a win.

Scatter (SC): Pays anywhere. 3 = 2× bet + Free Spins. 4 = 10× bet. 5 = 50× bet.

Golden Modifier (G01): Converts its cell golden; golden cells in a winning line convert to Bursting Wild.

Bonus (Wheel): 3 on reels 1 / 3 / 5 triggers the Wheel Feature.

FREE SPINS FEATURE

3+ Scatters anywhere award 10 Free Spins. During FS the multiplier ladder advances faster: ×2 → ×4 → ×6 → ×10.

Retrigger: 3 scatters during FS add +5 spins, 4 add +8, 5 add +10. Maximum 100 total FS per session.

Bet is locked to the triggering bet during Free Spins.

MULTIPLIER CHAIN

Base game: Each winning cascade step advances the multiplier — ×1 → ×2 → ×3 → ×5.

Free Spins: ×2 → ×4 → ×6 → ×10.

The multiplier resets at the start of every new spin and does not persist between base game and Free Spins.

WHEEL FEATURE

Three Bonus symbols on reels 1, 3 and 5 trigger the Wheel Feature. The wheel has 7 segments:

  • Jackpot Grand (1%) — Grand jackpot 2000× stake
  • Jackpot Major (4%) — Major jackpot prize
  • Jackpot Minor (12%) — Minor jackpot prize
  • Jackpot Mini (8%) — Mini jackpot prize
  • Pearl Cave Bonus (30%) — Pick-me underwater prize game
  • Kraken Bonus (25%) — Tentacles reveal ocean prizes
  • Mermaid Bonus (20%) — Enchanted reels spin for big wins

The Wheel Feature can also be purchased — see BUY FEATURE.

BUY FEATURE

Buy Free Spins: Costs 75× your current bet. Triggers 10 Free Spins immediately.

Buy Wheel: Costs 50× your current bet. Triggers an instant Wheel spin.

Not available during Autoplay or while a feature is active.

PAYLINES

10 fixed paylines, left-to-right only, starting from reel 1. A minimum of 3 matching symbols on a payline is required for a win. Only the highest win per payline is paid.
